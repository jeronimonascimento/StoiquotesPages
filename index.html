Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Stoiquotes is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

The short version: we collect as little as possible, we never sell your data, and your reading habits stay on your device.

Information We Collect

Stoiquotes does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration is required
  • No personal data is uploaded to our servers
  • Your favorites, notes, and reading history are stored locally on your device only
  • We do not use advertising identifiers or tracking pixels

Analytics

We may use Apple's App Store analytics, which is governed by Apple's own privacy policy. This provides aggregate, anonymized usage statistics (such as total installs and app crashes) and does not identify individual users.

We do not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs into Stoiquotes.

Data Sharing

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to share. We do not sell, trade, or transfer any user information to third parties.

Children's Privacy

Stoiquotes does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no age-restricted content and is suitable for general audiences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Support

Frequently asked
questions.

Is Stoiquotes free?

Yes. Stoiquotes is completely free to download and use. There are no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

Does the app require an internet connection?

An internet connection is required to load quotes, as they are fetched from our API. Make sure you're connected to enjoy the full experience.

Which philosophers are included?

The library covers the primary Stoic canon: Marcus Aurelius, Seneca, Epictetus, Zeno of Citium, Chrysippus, Musonius Rufus, Cleanthes, and Cato the Younger, among others.

How do I save a favorite quote?

Tap the bookmark icon on any quote card to add it to your favorites. Your saved quotes are stored locally on your device.

Can I share quotes with friends?

Yes. Every quote can be shared as a beautifully formatted image or plain text via the share button. The image uses the Stoiquotes visual identity and is designed to look great on any social platform.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. We do not collect any personal data. Your reading history, favorites, and notes never leave your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
